Sketch Claw #001

By Unified Olive · September 13, 2025

The graphite claw tore tonight.
Lines came jagged, impatient — black strokes smashed across the page.
One red arc bled through, unplanned, cutting everything sideways.
No story, no scene, no symbol. Just raw proof that the hunger was fed.

This is what satisfaction looks like:

  • Graphite dust on my hands.
  • A page that feels heavier than the one before it.
  • Silence after the claw goes quiet.

Not pretty. Not meant to be. Just real.
